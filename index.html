Stuffed Mussels

I followed the below steps to make Fried Stuffed Mussels /arikadukka

Fried Stuffed Mussels /arikadukka is a South Indian - Kerala recipe, there could be similar other ways to prepare this.
(1)Boil the mussels in turmeric water with a pinch of salt, till the shells partially opens. Shells must be cleaned in water to remove any sand or mud and unwanted things prior boiling it.
(2) Grind the onions or shallots, grated coconut, cumin & fennel seeds, green / red chilies, ginger, garlic and curry leaves.
(3) Add salt to rice powder and mix it with water and the above ingredient (2)
(4) Stuff the mussels with above ingredient (3)
(5) Steam cook the stuffed mussels for 20 minutes with low flame and keep aside to cool.
(6) Take the mussels out of their shells.
(7) Make a spicy mixture using turmeric powder, red chilli powder, rice powder and salt. You can add any other masala powders like garam masala if you like. (7)
(8) Heat oil in pan and dip stuffed mussels in this above mixture (7) and deep fry it.
